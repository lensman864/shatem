MODE OF OPERATION

1. Project concept and initiation: This start by collecting brief from the client as we believe the client always have an idea of what they want.

2. Design and Planning: The conversion of the brief into reality by creating a design for the project.

The design will be fully supported with 3D model visualization for comprehensive presentation to the client, depicting what the proposed project will look like at completion.

3. Project Approval: First, we shall secure client's approval on the design.

Once we get client's approval, we represent the bill of quantity and commence geotechnical and earthwork soil test to determine or confirm whether such project can be constructed on the particular soil.

4. Construction: When all this as been established.we start the processing the building approval on the project from the government, after the building plan has been approved, we are ready to move to site by client instruction.

5. Fixings and Finishes: Once the structure is completed, The interior design terms we will start work on the interior design and finishes
